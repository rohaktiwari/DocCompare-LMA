SECTION 8. REPRESENTATIONS, WARRANTIES AND UNDERTAKINGS

8.1 Financial Covenants
The Borrower shall ensure that:
(a) Leverage Ratio: The Leverage Ratio in respect of any Relevant Period shall not exceed 3.00:1.
(b) Interest Cover: The Interest Cover in respect of any Relevant Period shall not be less than 4.00:1.

8.2 Negative Pledge
No Obligor shall (and the Company shall ensure that no other member of the Group will) create or permit to subsist any Security over any of its assets.

8.3 Disposals
No Obligor shall (and the Company shall ensure that no other member of the Group will) enter into a single transaction or a series of transactions (whether related or not) and whether voluntary or involuntary to sell, lease, transfer or otherwise dispose of any asset.

SECTION 23. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

23.1 Non-payment
The Obligor does not pay on the due date any amount payable pursuant to a Finance Document at the place and in the currency in which it is expressed to be payable unless:
(a) its failure to pay is caused by administrative or technical error; and
(b) payment is made within 3 Business Days of its due date.

23.2 Cross default
Any Financial Indebtedness of any member of the Group is not paid when due nor within any originally applicable grace period.
