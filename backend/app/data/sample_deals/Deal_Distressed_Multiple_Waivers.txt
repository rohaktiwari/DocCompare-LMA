FACILITY AGREEMENT
dated October 2021
BORROWER: Troubled Retail Holdings Limited
THIRD AMENDMENT dated December 2024

SECTION 8. FINANCIAL COVENANTS (AS AMENDED)

8.1 Leverage Ratio [WAIVED THROUGH Q2 2025]
The Borrower shall ensure that the Leverage Ratio in respect of each Relevant Period shall not exceed:
- Q3 2024: 6.50:1 [BREACHED - Waived]
- Q4 2024: 6.00:1 [AMENDED from original 4.50:1]
- Q1 2025: 5.75:1 [AMENDED from original 4.00:1]
- Q2 2025: 5.50:1 [AMENDED from original 3.75:1]
- Q3 2025 onwards: 4.50:1

NOTE: Leverage covenant suspended for Q3 2024 pursuant to Waiver Letter dated September 15, 2024.

8.2 Interest Cover [AMENDED]
The Borrower shall ensure that the Interest Cover Ratio in respect of each Relevant Period shall not be less than 1.75:1.
[Original covenant: 4.00:1 - Amended via First Amendment dated March 2023]

8.3 Liquidity [NEW COVENANT ADDED]
The Borrower shall at all times maintain unrestricted cash and Cash Equivalents of not less than EUR 15,000,000.
[Added pursuant to Second Amendment dated June 2024 as condition of leverage covenant relaxation]

SECTION 23. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

23.1 Non-payment
An Event of Default occurs if the Obligor does not pay on the due date any amount payable pursuant to a Finance Document unless payment is made within 10 Business Days.
[Original grace period: 3 days - Extended via Third Amendment dated December 2024]

23.3 Cross default
An Event of Default occurs if any Financial Indebtedness exceeding EUR 20,000,000 is not paid when due.
[Original threshold: EUR 5,000,000 - Increased via Second Amendment]

- Third Amendment: December 2024 (Further leverage relaxation, extended grace period)
- Waiver Letter: September 2024 (Suspended Q3 2024 Leverage test)
